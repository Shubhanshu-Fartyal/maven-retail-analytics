# Business Insights

This document summarizes the key insights derived from the SQL analysis of the Global Electronics Retailer dataset.

---

## Business Problem 1
### Total Revenue

#### Key Insights

- The retailer generated a total revenue of **$55.76 million** during the analysis period.
- This serves as the baseline KPI for evaluating overall business performance.

---

## Business Problem 2
### Revenue by Year

#### Key Insights

- Revenue grew consistently from **2016** and peaked at **$18.26 million in 2019**.
- Revenue declined in **2020** compared to the previous year.
- **2021** recorded the lowest revenue because the dataset contains only partial-year data.

---

## Business Problem 3
### Revenue by Product Category

#### Key Insights

- **Computers** generated the highest revenue (**$19.30M**).
- **Home Appliances** ranked second, followed by **Cameras and Camcorders**.
- **Games and Toys** contributed the lowest revenue among all product categories.

---

## Business Problem 4
### Top 10 Products by Revenue

#### Key Insights

- **WWI Desktop PC2.33 X2330 Black** was the highest revenue-generating product.
- Desktop computers dominate the Top 10 revenue-generating products.
- Multiple color variants of the same desktop models appear among the top performers, indicating consistent customer demand.

---

## Business Problem 5
### Top 10 Products by Quantity Sold

#### Key Insights

- **WWI Desktop PC2.33 X2330 Black** sold the highest number of units (**550**).
- Desktop PC models occupy most of the Top 10 positions by quantity sold.
- Products with the highest sales volume are not always the highest revenue-generating products.

---

## Business Problem 6
### Brand-wise Revenue

#### Key Insights

- **Adventure Works** generated the highest brand revenue (**$11.85M**).
- **Contoso** and **Wide World Importers** ranked second and third respectively.
- Revenue is concentrated among a few leading brands, while the remaining brands contribute comparatively less.

---

## Business Problem 7
### Top 10 Customers by Spending

#### Key Insights

- **Matthew Flemming** was the highest-spending customer, contributing over **$61K** in revenue.
- The Top 10 customers each spent between **$36K and $62K**.
- These customers represent valuable opportunities for customer retention and loyalty initiatives.

---

## Business Problem 8
### Revenue by Country

#### Key Insights

- The **United States** generated the highest revenue (**$29.87M**), contributing more than half of the total revenue.
- **United Kingdom** and **Germany** were the next highest revenue-generating markets.
- The retailer generated sales across **8 countries**, demonstrating a broad international presence.

---

## Business Problem 9
### Customer Distribution by Continent

#### Key Insights

- **North America** has the largest customer base with **8,381 customers**.
- **Europe** ranks second with **5,465 customers**.
- **Australia** has the smallest customer base with **1,420 customers**.

---

## Business Problem 10
### Top Performing Stores

#### Key Insights

- The **Online Store** generated the highest revenue (**$11.40M**) among all stores.
- Most of the top-performing physical stores are located in the **United States**.
- Revenue is concentrated among a relatively small number of high-performing stores.

---

## Business Problem 11
### Online vs Physical Store Performance

#### Key Insights

- **Physical Stores** generated **$44.35M**, accounting for nearly **80%** of total revenue.
- The **Online Store** contributed **$11.40M**, representing approximately **20%** of total revenue.
- Physical stores remain the company's primary sales channel, while online sales provide a significant secondary revenue stream.

---

## Business Problem 12
### Top 5 Products within Each Category

#### Key Insights

- Every product category has a distinct set of high-performing products based on revenue.
- Desktop PCs dominate the **Computers** category, while **LCD HDTVs** lead the **TV and Video** category.
- A small number of flagship products contribute a significant share of revenue within each category.
