# Business Problems

This project analyzes the sales performance of a global electronics retailer using PostgreSQL. The objective is to answer key business questions that help management understand sales performance, product trends, customer behavior, and store performance.

---

## Business Problem 1
### Total Revenue

Determine the total revenue generated during the analysis period.

---

## Business Problem 2
### Revenue by Year

Analyze yearly revenue trends to understand business growth over time.

---

## Business Problem 3
### Revenue by Product Category

Identify the product categories contributing the highest revenue.

---

## Business Problem 4
### Top 10 Products by Revenue

Find the highest revenue-generating products to identify the company's best-performing products.

---

## Business Problem 5
### Top 10 Products by Quantity Sold

Identify the most frequently purchased products based on quantity sold.

---

## Business Problem 6
### Brand-wise Revenue

Compare revenue generated by each brand to evaluate brand performance.

---

## Business Problem 7
### Top 10 Customers by Spending

Identify the customers contributing the highest revenue.

---

## Business Problem 8
### Revenue by Country

Analyze revenue across countries to identify the strongest markets.

---

## Business Problem 9
### Customer Distribution by Continent

Understand the geographical distribution of customers.

---

## Business Problem 10
### Top Performing Stores

Identify stores generating the highest revenue.

---

## Business Problem 11
### Online vs Physical Store Performance

Compare the sales performance of online and physical stores.

---

## Business Problem 12
### Top 5 Products within Each Category

Rank products within every category to identify category leaders.
